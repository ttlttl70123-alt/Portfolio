It is a brand that started in Italy in 1967. After being acquired by the Chinese Biem.L.Fdlkk Group, they proceeded with a rebranding and requested a new S.I from us. The main request was to make it look like a typical "luxury store." First, we analyzed the roots of the brand. The founder, Nino Cerruti, is close to being the creator of the genderless look. Therefore, we inherited his legacy by intentionally using contrasting materials in the space while simultaneously making them blend together. For the lighting, we incorporated subtle lighting throughout the space as much as possible. It is configured to have minimal direct lighting, yet well-secured illumination, making it luxurious while allowing the gaze to naturally stay on the products. Although it is not a very large space, due to the nature of the brand, we made the space flexibly usable at any time when VIP customers visit. It can be used as a spacious area usually, but can be used as a personal private room when necessary. When thinking about what a luxury store is, I thought that luxury is not about making a space feel difficult to visit. Rather, it is about allowing customers to come in comfortably, experience various products, and leading them to a natural and comfortable purchase. Therefore, we configured the store so that a comfortable, home-like atmosphere melts into it.
